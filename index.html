Welcome to RPG Studio!

1. Drawing: Select a Terrain block on the left. Click and drag on the grid to draw the world.

2. Placing Enemies: Click the "Entities" tab on the left. Click on the map to place Spawns, Slimes, or Orcs. You must place 1 Player Spawn to play!

3. Moving the Camera: Click the "Pan" tool at the top, OR hold the Middle Mouse Button and drag to move around the large map. Use your Scroll Wheel to zoom.

4. Playtesting: Click the green "Playtest" button to instantly play your game. Use WASD or Arrows to move. Auto-attack handles combat.
